How to Play

General Idea

Play the game, gather points, unlock new healthy foods and learn more about them.

Play

This section contains two game modes: Food Association and Word Association.

For each correct answer, you get points. The faster you answer, the more points you score. In total, there are 35 foods you can unlock. Each time you unlock a food, information on the food and its health benefits is displayed to you.

If you manage to answer correctly to each food or word displayed during a game session, you get a bonus. Additionally, if your answers are quick enough, you get an even bigger bonus!

My Profile

In My Profile, you can monitor your progress by viewing charts of your performance, browsing gameplay statistics, and checking your current points and level. From here, you can also re-read information about each food you have unlocked.
